    Glide: A Minecraft Minigame

    For my first internship at Minecraft, I was tasked with recreating an existing mini-game called Glide, in the Bedrock edition of Minecraft. I worked with one other intern, and I was responsible for the staging of the player before the game started. This included programming a countdown timer, restricting movement of the player, creating a new gamemode for Glide, as well as filling the player's inventory with approriate items.
